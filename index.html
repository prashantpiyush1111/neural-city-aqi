Dataset & Methodology

Data Sources & Cleaning Process

Dataset Used CPCB Annual AQI Bulletin 2023 — compiled by urbanemissions.info from official CPCB PDF bulletins. Available at data.gov.in (Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change). Day-wise, city-average AQI values across 271 cities.
Why This Dataset Neural City measures physical urban quality — roads, footpaths, cleanliness. AQI adds the invisible environmental layer. A city can look clean on street cameras but have hazardous PM2.5. This overlay creates a complete liveability index.
Cleaning Steps 1. Filtered day-wise CSV to cities present in Neural City ranking. 2. Dropped city-years with <180 days of valid readings. 3. Computed annual mean AQI per city. 4. Normalized city names (e.g., "Bengaluru" vs "Bangalore"). 5. Output: aqi_2023.json
Risk Gap Formula gap = (streetScore/100) − (1 − AQI/300)
Positive gap = city performs better on streets than air quality. High gap = municipal blind spot. Helps prioritize where environmental investment should follow infrastructure investment.
